

Fry venison until brown. Season with salt and olive oil. 
Put in a casserole dish in the oven at 200 degrees for 2 hours. 
Boil the brussels sprouts.  Cut the mozzarella in pieces. 
Put the venison and the brussels sprouts on a plate and the 
mozzarella pieces above them. Put the plate into the microwave 
for about a minute, until the mozzarella has melted. Voila!
